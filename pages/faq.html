How do I report technical errors and bugs?
  • If you encounter any technical errors, bugs, or issues on the Dear Anon website, please follow these guidelines to ensure your report is reviewed :

  • Email Us : Send your report to dearanon.heartfelt@gmail.com.
  • Subject Line : Your email subject MUST be "Dear Anon Website Technical Error Report".
  • Describe the Issue : Clearly explain the bug, error, or issue you've found.
  • Steps to Reproduce (if possible) : If you can, describe the step-by-step process that leads to the error. This helps us understand and fix it faster.
  • Include Visuals : You MUST include screenshots or videos that show the error.

  • Please note that reports not following these guidelines may not be reviewed.
